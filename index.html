Need to Download or Install? Find Solutions at Setup.Brother.com

Learn how to easily download and install Brother products with step-by-step guides and helpful tips available at Setup.Brother.com

  • Visit the Official Website: Navigate to Setup.Brother.com to access the official Brother support page.
  • Select Your Product: Choose your specific Brother product model from the list provided.
  • Download the Software:
    Locate the 'Downloads' section for your product.
    Select the appropriate software or driver compatible with your operating system.
    Click on the 'Download' button to start the download process.
  • Installation Guide:
    Once the download is complete, locate the downloaded file on your computer.
    Double-click the file to begin the installation.
    Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation process.
  • Restart Your Device: After installation, restart your computer or device to ensure the software functions correctly.
  • Test the Installation:
    Open the installed Brother software.
    Verify that your Brother product is working properly.

How do I set up a Brother machine and my mobile device using Setup.brother.com on a wireless (Wi-Fi) network?



  • Preparation:
    Brother Machine: Ensure your Brother machine is powered on and located within the range of your Wi-Fi network.
    Wi-Fi Network Information: Have your Wi-Fi network name (SSID) and password ready.
  • Connect Brother Machine to Wi-Fi:
    Access Settings: On your Brother machine, go to the "Menu" or "Settings" option.
    Network Configuration: Navigate to the "Network" or "Wireless Setup" section.
    Wi-Fi Setup: Choose "Wi-Fi Setup Wizard" to begin the setup process.
    Select Network: From the available networks, select your Wi-Fi network.
    Enter Password: Input the Wi-Fi password when prompted to establish the connection.
  • Access Setup.brother.com on Mobile Device:
    Open Web Browser: Launch the web browser on your mobile device.
    Enter Website: Type "Setup.brother.com" into the address bar and hit Enter to access the setup page.
  • Follow On-Screen Instructions:
    Installation Guidance: The Setup.brother.com website will provide step-by-step instructions.
    Download Driver: You may be prompted to download the Brother printer driver on your mobile device.
    Complete Setup: Adhere to the on-screen prompts and instructions to finalize the setup process.
  • Test the Connection:
    Print Test Pages: After the setup is complete, print a test page from both the Brother machine and your mobile device.
    Check Connectivity: Ensure that both devices are connected and functioning properly on the same wireless network.
